A dab of greasepaint... A simple wig... A swatch of fabric... The artiste sprinkles some creative pixie-dust and waves his magic wand of imagination. Presto! You have been transformed from ordinary and forgettable into someone who commands attention. Such is the magic of stage makeup and costuming.
Outer Beauty...

Taking what he has learned in the dressing room, Chris is excited to bring his artistic tools to a wider audience. Since beauty is only skin deep, Chris believes that true happiness is found by improving external appearance. Not happy with who you are? Feeling unloved or lonely? It's probably because you are aesthetically-challenged. Chris will work with you, not only to cover up the blemishes of your soul, but also to teach you the tools to keep yourself looking beautiful.
