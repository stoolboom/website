Many authors pen books on love and romance. Precious few though, try to grapple with the sweeping breadth of this most powerful of human emotions. It takes a special talent to weave the multi-colored threads of amour into a glorious tapestry that expresses the entire essence of love, and distill it into a 144 page romance novel. Chris Dunlop is that talent; effortlessly moving between primal, gutteral passion, and deep reverence for sacrificial love - often in the same sentence.

The McGonogal Chronicles

Chris' beloved romance series, The McGonogal Chronicles, transports you back in time to nineteenth century rural Ireland, where a man is a man, and life is as cold and hard as the Blarney stone. Against this backdrop, follow lass Fiona McGonogal as she navigates the tempestous tides from adolescence to womanhood.

 You've loved the book, now see it as a Broadway stage production!
