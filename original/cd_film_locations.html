Growing up on stage left, Chris knows firsthand the impression that Theatre can leave on youngsters' minds. He feels a personal responsibility to shepherd and mentor the next generation of theatre performers.

With this in mind, Chris has developed series of musicals tailored specifically to meet the needs of young performers. Each musical comes packaged with extensive resource materials designed to foster holistic growth - artistic, character, and spiritual - in the lives of each impressionable youngster.

Introducing the latest entry in the Aim! High! Lil! Squirts! catalog...

The complete Deliverance - The Musical† package contains everything you need to stage this backwoods romance, including:

  • Split-track Practice CD, including full company versions of:
    • Squeal Like A Pig (Weeeeeeee!)
    • Purty Mouth
    • Banjo-Boy-Boogie
  • Makeup Kit including:
    • Instructions for creating realistic genetic mutation effects
    • Highlight and shadow colors specially designed to mimic iron-deficient complexions
    • Removable front teeth dentures
  • Discussion Guide, What did happen on the Cahulawassee River?   
    This excellent companion book will foster group discussion on such relevant topics as:
    • Were the rafters right to feel violated, or was it just a cultural misunderstanding?
    • Is there anything they could have done differently?
    • What would you do if faced with a similar situation?
    • What are the positives you can take away from this story?
†This show includes graphic violence and mature themes. Not suitable for children.
