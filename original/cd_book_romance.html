Many authors pen books on love and romance. Precious few though, even try to grapple with the sweeping breadth of this most powerful of human emotions. In his romantic writings, Chris Dunlop untangles the multi-colored threads of amour and weaves them into a glorious literary tapestry that depicts the very fullness of love. Few others can navigate the tempestuous seas of romance as Chris, moving effortlessly between primal, gutteral passion, and deep reverence for sacrificial love - often in the same sentence.

The McGonogal Chronicles

Chris' beloved romance series, The McGonogal Chronicles, transports you back in time to nineteenth century rural Ireland, where a man is a man, and life is as cold and hard as the Blarney stone. Against this backdrop, follow lass Fiona McGonogal as she navigates the tempestous tides from adolescence to womanhood.

Agonize with her as she must choose between steadfast devotion and selfish indulgence in Book One, Tides of the Heart.   Flee with her northward, where frenzied ecstasy waits in ambush on the Scottish moor, in Book Two, A Highland Entanglement.   Escape with her across the western sea, where a captain's billowing passion threatens to engulf her, in Book Three, Her Booty to Plunder.
